About Us

"Navigating the world of valuation requires a trusted partner"

We are a team of experienced professionals that truly understand your valuation needs. Our diverse background places us from the perspectives of listed companies, private enterprises, investment companies and up-and-coming ventures.

We aim to provide you holistic valuation advisory and business consultancy services, enabling your business to thrive and realise your strategic objectives. Our services can be applied to M&A, financial reporting, investment and business expansion and planning purposes.

Our Services

“We offer solutions according to your needs”

ROYSON provides valuation services that facilitate corporate decision-making process and financial management, as well as intangible assets and financial derivatives valuation that shapes your business landscape.

We offer tailored advice on decisions such as financing major projects, making mergers and acquisitions, public listing, strategic joint-ventures. We also provide services for your accounting use.

Business Valuation

We conduct market and industry research across different business sectors and geographic areas. Our database and technical modelling enable us to make quantitative and qualitative analysis of your business.

Intangible Assets

We value intellectual properties include but not limited to trademarks, patents, copyrights, know-how, license agreements, assembled workforce, customer relationship and distribution networks via disciplined valuation methodologies.

Financial Derivatives

We value various sophisticated financial instruments such as share options and warrants, convertible bonds and preference shares, financial guarantees, Swap via appropriate financial modelling.

Property valuation

We appraise your properties such as land, offices, carparks, industrial plants as well as residential apartments and houses to maximize your property portfolio and structure your strategy.

Plant and Machinery

We value different plant and machinery across different manufacturing industries, especially in China, such as telecommunication and production facilities, etc.
